Our Story

Surge AutoTech Limited is a family owned and operated automotive repair shop established in 2018. We specialize in all makes and models of vehicles and our auto repairs are often started and finished in the same day!

You know it will be done right the first time with over eight years experience previously working at both Toyota and Honda New Zealand. We set our standards high to ensure satisfaction every visit.

Advanced computer systems, record keeping & thorough vehicle inspections allow you to get the most out of your investment.
